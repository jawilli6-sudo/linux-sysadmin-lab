=== TECHNICAL NOTE: RHEL 9 CRYPTO POLICY OVERRIDE ===
RHEL 9 uses a system-wide crypto policy loaded via /etc/ssh/sshd_config.d/50-redhat.conf.
This file overrides cipher/MAC/KEX settings written directly to /etc/ssh/sshd_config.
To enforce STIG-required algorithms, a drop-in file was created at:
  /etc/ssh/sshd_config.d/01-stig-crypto.conf
Files in sshd_config.d/ load alphabetically. The 01- prefix ensures STIG settings
load before and take precedence over the 50-redhat.conf system defaults.
This is the correct approach for STIG hardening on RHEL 9 systems.
